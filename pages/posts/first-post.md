Welcome to my portfolio. I'm starting this site to document the projects
I'm working on and the things I'm learning along the way. Expect short
posts about tools, workflows, and the occasional deep dive into a
problem worth solving.

# Why a portfolio

A portfolio is more than a list of past work. It's a record of how I
think, what I value, and where I'm spending my time. Building this site
in public also gives me a reason to keep writing, even when the topics
are small.

More posts to come. If something here is useful to you, that's a happy
accident — and if it's not, that's fine too.
